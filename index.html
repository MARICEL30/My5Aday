A list of the most popular veggies and their nutrients.

The health benefits of having fruits and veggies

in our diets are very well- known.

The World Health Organization (WHO) says eating at

least 400 g of fruits and veggies per day will lower

the risk of heart desease, stroke and some types of cancer.

The NHS gives a detailed explanation on how to incorporate

veggies and fruits in our daily meals.

Eating healthy doesn't need to be complicated.

We listed the most important veggies.

Look up the ones you love and combine them the way you like.
